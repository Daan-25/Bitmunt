What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that operates without a central bank. It was created in 2009 by an unknown person or group using the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto.

Benefits

  • Decentralized
  • Low transaction fees
  • Fast international payments
  • Transparent blockchain

How It Works

Bitcoin operates on a blockchain - a distributed ledger that records all transactions. Miners verify transactions by solving complex mathematical problems.
